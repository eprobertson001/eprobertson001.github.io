Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Simple websites typically take 2-4 weeks, while custom applications can take 2-6 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our consultation.

Do you work with nonprofits?

Absolutely! I offer a 20% discount for registered nonprofits and have experience building donor management systems, volunteer coordination tools, and other mission-critical applications.

What happens after the project is complete?

You receive all source code, documentation, and training materials. I provide 30 days of free support and am available for ongoing maintenance and updates at competitive rates.

Can you integrate with existing systems?

Yes, I specialize in creating solutions that work with your current tools. Whether it's accounting software, CRM systems, or payment processors, integration is often possible.

What if I'm not sure what I need?

That's perfectly normal! During our free consultation, I'll help you identify the best solutions for your specific challenges and goals. No technical expertise required on your part.

Do you offer hosting and maintenance?

Yes, I can provide hosting recommendations and ongoing maintenance services. However, you always own your code and data, so you're never locked into any particular provider.

Custom Inventory Management System

Retail 3 months

The Challenge

A local bookstore was struggling with manual inventory tracking using spreadsheets. They were experiencing frequent stockouts, overstocking issues, and spent hours each week on manual counts and reorder calculations.

The Solution

Developed a lightweight web-based inventory system with barcode scanning, automated reorder alerts, and simple reporting. The system integrated with their existing POS system and provided real-time stock levels.

Results

  • Reduced inventory management time by 80%
  • Eliminated stockouts of popular items
  • Decreased excess inventory by 30%
  • Improved cash flow through better purchasing decisions

Nonprofit Donor Management Tool

Nonprofit 2 months

The Challenge

  • Improved campaign response rates by 25%
  • Saved 10 hours per week on administrative tasks
